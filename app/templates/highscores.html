How do some people have so many blocks?

Unfortunately, some people have taken to "pooling" Guardian Masternode and Masternode wallets in order to gain more WTCT per block. This means that multiple people will mine to a GMN wallet address and expect to be paid back a portion of each block that they mine. For example, instead of recieveing 2 WTCT per block, a GMN will recieve 4 and will then split the extra 2 WTCT with the miner.
We at Easy Walton Miner DO NOT reccommend or endorse these services due to the risky nature of trusting unknown third parties. Any requests to implement or otherise help with this kind of unofficial pool will not be responded to.
